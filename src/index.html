Services

Sports massage uses precisely targeted massage techniques to enhance soft tissue health, reduce tension, improve mobility and alleviate pain.

Each treatment is different and will be tailored to you. I link many techniques together, depending on your needs and the goals we've discussed.

These techniques include:

  • Effleurage and lymphatic drainage
  • Petrissage
  • Tapotement
  • Deep friction techniques
  • Trigger pointing
  • Muscle energy techniques
  • Passive and dynamic stretching

If your symptoms are preventing you from activity or movement, sports massage can help.
